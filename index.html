    Indira Nooyi, Buisness Executive

    More Information

    Who is she?

    Indira Nooyi is an Indian-American Business executive who started her career in India at a British Textile company. She started marketing in innovative ways. She moved to USA after a while for buisness studies. She was courted by many corporate companies. She worked at PepsiCo as CEO for more than a decade. Now she serves on the board of Amazon.

    Why is Indra Nooyi is my inspiration?

    Awards & Achievements

    As an employee of PepsiCo, Nooyi is credited to have directed the company’s global strategy, leading to its extensive expansion and restructuring including the divestiture of its restaurants. She played an instrumental role in PepsiCo’s acquisition of Tropicana and merger with Quaker Oats Company.

    - Major work of Indira Nooyi

    She has consistently ranked on ‘Forbes’ magazine’s list of The World's 100 Most Powerful Women. ’Fortune’ magazine named Indra Nooyi No.1 on its annual ranking of Most Powerful Women in business for five consecutive years from 2006 to 2010.

    - Listed on Forbes

    She was honored with the Padma Bhushan by the President of India in 2007. She has been named 2009 CEO of the Year by Global Supply Chain Leaders Group.

    - Awards & Achievement

    Personal Life

    She was born as Indra Krishnamurthy on 28 October 1955, in Madras (now Chennai), Tamil Nadu, India. She is married to Raj Nooyi, a management consultant of Indian origin. The couple has two daughters. In spite of her very hectic corporate career, Indra always prioritizes her family and maintains close relations with her mother.
